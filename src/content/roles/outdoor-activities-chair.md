## Typical responsibilities

- Help plan outings and pack activities
- Coordinate approved public event descriptions
- Support safety planning and family communication
- Keep exact private travel details off the public site

Use the [Event Planning Checklist](/resources/event-planning-checklist/) and current Scouting safety guidance when planning outings.
