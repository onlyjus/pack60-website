## Typical responsibilities

- Assist with pack meeting planning and delivery
- Help lead activities when the Cubmaster is unavailable
- Support den leaders and families
- Take on assigned program tasks

## Helpful resources

Use [My Scouting](https://my.scouting.org/) for required training and coordinate role expectations with the Cubmaster and committee chair.
