## Typical responsibilities

- Plan age-appropriate den meetings
- Coordinate with families in the den
- Help scouts work through rank activities
- Share meeting details with den families

## Helpful resources

Use [My Scouting](https://my.scouting.org/) for required training and the [Den Meeting Planning Guide](/resources/den-meeting-planning-guide/) for meeting planning.
