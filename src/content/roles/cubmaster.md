## Typical responsibilities

- Plan and lead pack meetings
- Encourage den leaders and scouts
- Help keep pack activities fun, safe, and age-appropriate
- Coordinate with the committee chair and other volunteers

## Helpful resources

Use [My Scouting](https://my.scouting.org/) for required training and Scouting America's [Cub Scout Adventures](https://www.scouting.org/programs/cub-scouts/adventures/) pages for program planning.
