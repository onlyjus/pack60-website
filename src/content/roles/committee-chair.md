## Typical responsibilities

- Lead committee meetings
- Support adult volunteer coordination
- Help set pack priorities and annual planning
- Work with the Cubmaster and chartered organization representative

## Helpful resources

Use [My Scouting](https://my.scouting.org/) for required training and the pack's public [leader resources](/leader-resources/) for planning guidance.
