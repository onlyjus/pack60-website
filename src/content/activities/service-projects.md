## What it is

Service projects help scouts practice helpfulness, citizenship, and care for the community.

## When it usually happens

Service opportunities may happen throughout the year as approved projects are planned.

## Why scouts enjoy it

Scouts can see that their work matters, learn teamwork, and build pride in helping others.

## What families should know

Service projects are planned around age-appropriate work and family participation.
