## What it is

Pack campouts are family-centered outdoor experiences with age-appropriate activities, shared meals, and simple outdoor skills.

## When it usually happens

Camping is often planned in fall or spring when weather and pack schedules allow.

## Why scouts enjoy it

Scouts enjoy sleeping outdoors, exploring nature, learning camp skills, spending time with friends, and trying new things with family support.

## What families should know

Campouts are planned with safety and family participation in mind. Public pages should list only general date ranges and approved public information. Exact campsite details, travel plans, and participant information belong in private pack communications.
