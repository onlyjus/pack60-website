## What it is

Blue & Gold is a pack celebration that brings families together to recognize the spirit of Cub Scouting, volunteer effort, and scout growth.

## When it usually happens

Many packs hold Blue & Gold in late winter. Exact Pack 60 plans should be shared only after approval for public posting.

## Why scouts enjoy it

Scouts enjoy the ceremony, food, decorations, skits, songs, recognition, and time with their den and pack.

## What families should know

This is usually a family event. Public pages should keep details general and avoid private location, attendee, or youth-identifying information.
