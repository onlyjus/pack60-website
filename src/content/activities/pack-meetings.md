## What it is

Pack meetings bring all dens together for activities, ceremonies, announcements, recognition, and family participation.

## When it usually happens

Pack 60 meets Tuesdays at 7:00 PM at [Suncrest United Methodist Church](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Suncrest+United+Methodist+Church%2C+Morgantown%2C+WV) in Morgantown, WV. Schedule changes and special event details are shared through official pack communication channels.

## Why scouts enjoy it

Scouts see friends from other dens, participate in larger activities, and celebrate progress.

## What families should know

Parents and guardians should expect to stay, participate, and help their scouts. Families visiting for the first time should contact the pack before attending so a volunteer can confirm arrival details.
