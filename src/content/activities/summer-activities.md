## What it is

Summer activities can include outdoor meetups, hikes, service, council activities, and informal family-friendly pack events.

## When it usually happens

Summer plans vary each year and should be confirmed through official pack communications.

## Why scouts enjoy it

Scouts get to keep friendships active, spend time outside, and try activities that fit warm-weather schedules.

## What families should know

Summer activities vary by year and are usually lighter than the school-year schedule.
