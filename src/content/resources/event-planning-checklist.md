title: 'Pack Event Planning Checklist'
description: 'Planning prompts for pack activities and events.'
category: 'Leaders'
order: 8
public: true
lastUpdated: '2026-05-12'
---

- Confirm the event purpose.
- Identify the adult lead.
- Confirm safety and supervision expectations.
- Decide which details families need before the event.
- Prepare supplies and setup needs.
- Share full details with registered families.
- Review photos before posting.
