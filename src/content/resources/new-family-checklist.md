- Read the Join page.
- Contact the pack at [membership@pack60.org](mailto:membership@pack60.org).
- Ask about the next approved visit opportunity.
- Review uniform basics before buying optional items.
- Use official registration links from pack leadership.
- Keep private youth and family information out of public channels.
