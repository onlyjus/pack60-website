Prefer photos of activities, gear, flags, crafts, cars, rockets, tents, hikes, service projects, and wide group scenes.

Avoid close-up portraits of individual youth. Do not identify youth by full name. Remove photos promptly when a parent or guardian requests removal.

Use the full policy at [Privacy / Public Content Policy](/privacy/).
