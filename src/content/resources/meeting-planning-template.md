Use this outline as a public-safe planning starter:

- Opening activity
- Gathering or game
- Main activity
- Skill or adventure connection
- Recognition or reflection
- Closing
- Supplies needed
- Adult helpers needed

Keep private attendance, behavior notes, and youth-specific information out of public files.
