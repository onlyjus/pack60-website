## Grade level

Tigers are typically first-grade scouts.

## General focus

Tiger activities build confidence through exploration, simple skills, family involvement, and den teamwork.

## Example activities

- Outdoor discovery
- Simple service
- Den games
- Crafts and projects
- Pack traditions

## Parent involvement

Adult partners are an important part of the Tiger experience.
