## Grade level

Lions are typically kindergarten scouts.

## General focus

The Lion year introduces scouting through short, active, family-centered activities.

## Example activities

- Simple games
- Nature walks
- Crafts
- Helping activities
- Pack meeting participation

## Parent involvement

Parents and guardians are closely involved in the Lion program and should expect to participate with their scout.

## Official resources

Use Scouting America's [Lion Adventures](https://www.scouting.org/programs/cub-scouts/adventures/lion/) page for official Lion program information.

Families receive den-specific meeting details through regular pack communications.
