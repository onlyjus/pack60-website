## Grade level

Webelos are typically fourth-grade scouts.

## General focus

Webelos scouts begin preparing for more independent outdoor skills, leadership, and the transition toward a troop.

## Example activities

- Outdoor planning
- Fitness and wellness
- Practical skills
- Service
- Troop visits when approved and coordinated by pack leadership

## Parent involvement

Parents and guardians continue supporting scouts while encouraging age-appropriate responsibility.

## Official resources

Use Scouting America's [Webelos Adventures](https://www.scouting.org/programs/cub-scouts/adventures/webelos/) page for official Webelos program information.

Families receive den-specific meeting details through regular pack communications.
