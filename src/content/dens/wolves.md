## Grade level

Wolves are typically second-grade scouts.

## General focus

The Wolf year adds more hands-on skills, simple leadership, service, and outdoor learning.

## Example activities

- Outdoor basics
- Safety skills
- Service projects
- Games and teamwork
- Creative projects

## Parent involvement

Parents and guardians support den activities, help scouts stay engaged, and assist leaders.

## Official resources

Use Scouting America's [Wolf Adventures](https://www.scouting.org/programs/cub-scouts/adventures/wolf/) page for official Wolf program information.

Families receive den-specific meeting details through regular pack communications.
