## Grade level

Arrow of Light scouts are typically fifth graders.

## General focus

Arrow of Light focuses on leadership, outdoor readiness, service, and preparing scouts for the next Scouting program.

## Example activities

- Outdoor skills
- Leadership practice
- Service
- Patrol-style teamwork
- Transition preparation

## Parent involvement

Families help scouts finish the Cub Scout journey and prepare for what comes next.

## Official resources

Use Scouting America's [Arrow of Light Adventures](https://www.scouting.org/programs/cub-scouts/adventures/arrow-of-light/) page for official Arrow of Light program information.

Families receive den-specific meeting details through regular pack communications.
