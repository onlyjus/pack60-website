## Grade level

Arrow of Light scouts are typically fifth graders.

## General focus

Arrow of Light focuses on leadership, outdoor readiness, service, and preparing scouts for the next Scouting program.

## Example activities

- Outdoor skills
- Leadership practice
- Service
- Patrol-style teamwork
- Transition preparation

## Parent involvement

Families help scouts finish the Cub Scout journey and prepare for what comes next.
