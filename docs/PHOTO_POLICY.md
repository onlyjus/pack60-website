# Photo Policy

Photos can make the site welcoming, but they must be safe for a public youth organization website.

## Approved photo types

- Group or activity photos
- Pinewood Derby cars and tracks
- Camping gear, tents, flags, and camp setups
- Crafts and activity supplies
- Rockets and STEM materials
- Service project supplies or finished work
- Hikes and outdoor scenes
- Wide event photos where youth are not individually identified

## Avoided photo types

- Close-up portraits of individual youth
- Photos identifying youth by full name
- Photos with visible private documents
- Photos showing private addresses
- Photos that reveal live outing locations
- Photos a parent or guardian has asked not to use

## Caption rules

Do not use youth full names. Do not include youth last names. Keep captions general and activity-focused.

## Removal request process

If a parent, guardian, or pack leader requests removal, remove the photo promptly and review related pages for similar issues.

## Permission reminder

Confirm pack photo permission practices before posting photos. When unsure, use public-safe illustrations or photos without identifiable youth.
