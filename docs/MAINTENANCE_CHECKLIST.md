# Maintenance Checklist

Review the public site monthly or seasonally.

## Checklist

- Check the Join page for clarity, current meeting guidance, and any facts waiting on human approval.
- Confirm the calendar embed still works.
- Review generic leader contact aliases.
- Check photos for public safety.
- Remove or update outdated dates.
- Check for broken links.
- Review the privacy and public content policy.
- Archive old content that is no longer useful.
- Update recruiting information before joining season.
- Confirm no private youth, parent, roster, address, meeting link, payment, or advancement data has been added.
